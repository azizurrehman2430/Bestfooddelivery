Russian Salad

Russian Salad, also known as Olivier Salad, is a classic dish with variations across Eastern Europe. Its core ingredients typically include potatoes, eggs, carrots, pickles, onions, peas, and a mayonnaise-based dressing. This salad is a staple at gatherings and reflects a rich culinary tradition that blends simplicity with hearty flavors. Each region adds its own twist, but the comforting taste of the creamy, crunchy, and colorful mix remains a beloved favorite.
